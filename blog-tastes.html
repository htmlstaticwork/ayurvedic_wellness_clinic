The 6 Tastes: How to Balance Every Meal

Published: March 24, 2026 | By Dr. Amrita Singh

Ayurveda identifies six tastes (Rasas) through which we experience our food: Sweet, Sour, Salty, Pungent, Bitter, and Astringent. Each taste has a unique effect on our mind and body based on the elements it contains.

1. Sweet (Earth + Water)

Found in grains, fruits, and dairy, the sweet taste is cooling and grounding. It provides nourishment and comfort, essential for building tissues and longevity.

2. Sour (Earth + Fire)

Found in fermented foods and citrus fruits, the sour taste stimulates digestion and appetite.

Bringing it Together

A balanced Ayurvedic meal should ideally contain all six tastes. This ensures that the body receives a full spectrum of nutrients and elements.
