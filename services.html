Common Questions

Service FAQ

Everything you need to know about our traditional treatments and bookings.

How do I choose the right treatment?
We recommend starting with a **Dosha Assessment**. Our practitioners will analyze your constitution and suggest therapies specifically for your imbalances.
What should I wear for a massage?
Traditional treatments use large amounts of herbal oils. We provide hygiene-friendly garments, and we suggest wearing loose, comfortable clothing for after the session.
Are the treatments safe during pregnancy?
Some therapies are excellent while others should be avoided. Please consult with our specialists during booking for a tailored prenatal wellness plan.
Do you offer home-based services?
Currently, we focus on providing a serene, controlled clinic environment for the best experience. However, we do offer online consultations for dietary and lifestyle plans.
